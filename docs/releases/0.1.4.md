# i2Message 0.1.4

This release makes the live app safe and usable as the everyday build.

- Prevents fixture contacts and conversations from entering the live send path.
- Resolves the current Cmd+N recipient before sending, eliminating stale-recipient selection.
- Uses real selected and dropped files for attachments.
- Prevents duplicate sends while a message or reply action is already in flight.
- Keeps new-conversation sends on the pending thread until the real Messages conversation appears.
- Preserves draft text and attachments during manual Messages handoff.
- Opens older search results even when their conversation is outside the recent sidebar window.
- Recovers from permission grants without requiring a relaunch.
- Makes unsupported threaded replies an explicit Messages.app handoff instead of a fake local reply.
- Restores Contacts and Calendar hardened-runtime entitlements.
